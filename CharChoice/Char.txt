Squirtle - After birth, its back swells and hardens into a shell. It sprays a potent foam from its mouth. 
Charmander - The flame on its tail shows the strength of its life-force. If Charmander is weak, the flame also burns weakly.
Bulbasaur - For some time after its birth, it uses the nutrients that are packed into the seed on its back in order to grow.
